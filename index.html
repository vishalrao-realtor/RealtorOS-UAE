The 9% Corporate Tax Reality

Individual direct property ownership remains 100% tax-exempt in the UAE. However, large portfolios exceeding AED 375,000 in net profit held in standard commercial LLCs are liable for the 9% CT framework. We structure Ultra-High-Net-Worth acquisitions through DIFC or ADGM Private Foundations to ensure 'Tax Transparent' status, maintaining 0% liability across generations.

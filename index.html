Off-Plan vs. Ready Arbitrage

Understanding where true Return on Equity (ROE) resides in the current market cycle.

The Off-Plan Leverage (Capital Growth)

Capturing 75.3% of total market value, off-plan ROE comes from capital efficiency. Controlling an asset with a 10-20% downpayment means any appreciation by handover yields a massive return on deployed cash. The 9% Corporate Tax Reality

Individual ownership remains 100% tax-exempt. However, properties bought through standard mainland LLCs where net profit exceeds AED 375,000 are subject to the 9% CT. Short-term rentals at massive scale can cross into "commercial business activity," triggering both VAT (5%) and Corporate Tax liability.

DIFC / ADGM Foundations

The ultimate "Orphan" holding structure. It has no shareholders and never dies, meaning properties never go through probate. It legally separates you from personal liability, shields from foreign forced-heirship laws, and utilizes "Tax-Transparent" status to keep yields at 0%.
